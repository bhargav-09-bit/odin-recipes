Chicken Nuggets

Description:

Chicken nuggets are bite-sized pieces of chicken, typically breast meat, coated in a breading or batter and then cooked. This popular snack or meal component is designed for convenience and enjoyed by all ages. Common breading ingredients include breadcrumbs, flour, and spices, while cooking methods range from frying for a crispy texture to baking or air frying for healthier options.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken
  • breadcrumbs
  • flour
  • Eggs
  • Salt, Pepper, Spices
  • Oil

Steps:

  1. Cut chicken: Bite-sized pieces.
  2. Bread: Flour, egg, then breadcrumbs (seasoned).
  3. Cook: Fry, bake, or air fry until golden and cooked through.
  4. Serve: Hot with dipping sauce.
