Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken biryani is a beloved and iconic dish originating from the Indian subcontinent. It's a complex and flavorful rice dish that combines fragrant basmati rice with marinated chicken, a blend of aromatic spices, and often, caramelized onions.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Basmati rice
  • onions
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Spices
  • Oil/Ghee
  • Mint

Steps:

  1. Marinate the Chicken: Mix chicken pieces with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, spices, and salt. Let it sit for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Fry the Onions: Thinly slice onions and fry them until golden brown. Set aside.
  3. Cook the Rice: Partially cook basmati rice with whole spices and salt. Drain and set aside.
  4. Prepare the Base: In a heavy-bottomed pot, heat oil/ghee. Add whole spices, then the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is partially done.
  5. Layer the Biryani: Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Add fried onions, mint, cilantro, and saffron milk.
  6. Dum Cooking: Seal the pot tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld.
  7. Serve: Gently fluff the rice and serve hot.
